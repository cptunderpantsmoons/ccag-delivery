# Stream 1 LawVU Replacement - Requirements Intake

## Objective
Replace existing LawVU legal management platform with an AI-powered POC that can be demonstrated live.

## Required Inputs
- Current LawVU workflows (matter intake, tracking, search, reporting)
- User roles and approval paths
- Integration touchpoints (DMS, email, identity, billing)
- Security and retention requirements
- Top 5 pain points with measurable impact

## Interview Checklist
1. What business outcomes define "replacement-ready" for this POC?
2. Which workflows must be demonstrated end-to-end in the first demo?
3. What LawVU functions are mandatory versus acceptable to defer?
4. What data sources are approved for prototype access?
5. What constraints apply to hosting, access control, and audit logging?
6. What acceptance criteria determine Company satisfaction?
